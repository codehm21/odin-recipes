How to Make an Omelette

Omelette is a great easy to make meal!

Prep Time 1 mins
Cook Time 5 mins
Total Time 6 Mins
Servings 1 Serving

Ingredients

  • 2 Large Sized Eggs
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoon grated cheese, any kind
  • 2 tablespoon chopped basil, parsley, or herb of your choice

Method

  1. Prep the eggs

    2. In a bowl, beat the eggs with a fork.

  2. Melt the butter

    3. In a 8-inch non stick skillet over medium-low heat, melt the butter.

  3. Add the eggs and cook the omelette

    4. Add the eggs to the skillet without stirring until edges set. With a silicone
    spatula, push the edges towards the centre of the pan and tilt the pan so the uncooked
    eggs move to the edge.

  4. Fill the omelette

    5. Place the cheese, tomatoes and herbs in a line down the centre and cook for 1 minute
    longer or until the eggs are mostly set but still a little soft in the centre.

  5. Fold and place the omelette

    6. Slide the spatula around one side of the omelette at the edge to loosen it.
    Slip it under the eggs and use it to half the egg carefully, tilt and place in plate.
