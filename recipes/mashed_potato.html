Perfect Mashed Potato

Want creamy Mashed Potato? Use Maris Piper potatos with lots of butter and cream.

Prep Time 10 mins
Cook Time 25 mins
Total Time 35 Mins
Servings 4 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds (680 g) Maris Piper potatoes, peeled and cut lengthwise into quarters
  • 1.2 teaspoon salt
  • 4 tablespoons (60ml) heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons (30g) butter
  • 1/4 cup hot milk
  • salt and pepper

Method

  1. Cook the potato's

    2. place pealed and cut potatos into a medium sized pan.
    Add cold water and turn heat to high until water boils.
    Turn the heat to medium and let simmer for 30 minutes, or until soft.

  2. Drain and mash the potato's

    3. Drain mash and pour warm cream and butter over the potatos
    and mash with a potato masher. Then use a strong wooden spoon
    to beat that mash.
