Soft Boiled Eggs

Want all the health benefits from eating a Boiled Egg but still want that runny Yolk?

Prep Time 5 mins
Cook Time 10 mins
Total Time 15 Mins
Servings 3 Servings

Ingredients

  • 3 Medium Sized Eggs

Method

  1. Boil the eggs

    2. Fill a small sauce pan up with cold water and place the eggs theirein.
    set cooker heat to max placing the saucepan on the stove and set a timer for 10 minutes.

  2. Peeling the Eggs

    3. After 10 minutes drain the hot water and replace with cold water.
    After a few minutes take out the eggs and peel.
